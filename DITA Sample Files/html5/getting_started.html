Getting Started

How to download and setup BreakBuddy.

These steps show you how to install and setup BreakBuddy on your device.

  1. Visit the App Site for IOS or iPhone.
  2. Search for BreakBuddy and click Install.
  3. Open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to set up an account, once BreakBuddy is installed.
  4. Launch BreakBuddy and create a new account.
  5. Allow Notifications so BreakBuddy can remind you of your breaks.
