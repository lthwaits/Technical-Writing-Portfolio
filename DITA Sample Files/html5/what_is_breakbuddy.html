What is BreakBuddy?

BreakBuddy is a health and well-being app for IOS or and Apple devices.

BreakBuddy is an app that turns your device into a health buddy. You can use the app to incorporate breaks into your day, get stretch ideas, set music break options, and create reminders every hour to take care of your physical and mental health.

BreakBuddy makes it easier to make your health a priority. Getting Started
