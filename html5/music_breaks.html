Music Breaks

How to select a song for music breaks in BreakBuddy.

These steps show you how to select a song in BreakBuddy during the music break. BreakBuddy offers short music clips to energize you during breaks.

  1. Tap Music Break in the notification.
  2. Select a song when the playlist opens.
  3. Use the Next Song button to switch tracks.
